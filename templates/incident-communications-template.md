# Incident Communications Template

## Initial Notification

`[SEV-X] <service> incident declared at <time UTC>. Symptoms: <summary>. IC: <name>. Next update in 15 minutes.`

## 15-Minute Update

`[SEV-X] <service> update <time UTC>: current impact <summary>. Mitigation in progress: <action>. ETA to next milestone: <estimate>. Next update in 15 minutes.`

## Recovery Notification

`[SEV-X] <service> recovered at <time UTC>. Service metrics returned to baseline. Monitoring for regression for 15 minutes. Postmortem to follow by <date>.`

## Closure Note

`[SEV-X] <service> incident closed at <time UTC>. Total duration: <duration>. Root cause: <summary>. Action items: <ticket links>.`
